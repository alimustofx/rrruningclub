No Boundaries!

All Runners Welcome

Rooted in the streets of South Malang, our run crew is built for everyone from elite runners chasing PR to total beginners lacing up for the first time. We believe running isn’t just a sport, it’s a culture.

No dress codes, no gatekeeping—whether you show up in full race gear or vintage streetwear, whether you’re fast, slow, or just vibing—you’re one of us.

We run twice a week, keeping the energy high and the routes fresh. And every month, we go bigger—throwing a special event that blends movement, music, and community vibes.

This isn’t just a crew, it’s a space where you can push your limits, find your rhythm, and connect with people who move with purpose.

We’re here to break the stereotype
of what a runner “should” look like.

Come as you are, run how you want.

This is our lane and there’s always room for more.
